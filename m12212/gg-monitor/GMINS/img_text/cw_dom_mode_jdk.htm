Domain Mode and JDK — This screen comprises two panels:

On the left is a status tree that indicates the step in the installation process represented by the screen.

The right panel contains two sets of options, indicated by radio buttons. The top two items on the list are under the header Domain Node. The options are labeled, top tp bottom, Development and Production. "Production" is selected. are three edit boxes labeled, top to bottom, Name, Password, and Confirm Password.

The third and fourth options are under the heading JDK. The first option is describes the available Oracle Hotspot JDK; the second option is labeled Other JDK Location and contains an edit box followed by a button labeled Browse.

At the bottom of this panel is a space reserved for system messages. There is text in this space.

At the bottom right of the screen is a Help button. At the bottom left are four buttons labeled, left to right, Back, Next, Finish, and Cancel. In this example, Finish is disabled.
