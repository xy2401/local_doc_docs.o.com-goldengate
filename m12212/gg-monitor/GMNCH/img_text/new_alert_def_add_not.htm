The Additional Notice Attributes section of the New Alerts Definition screen. This section is comprised of two list panels. The panel on the left contains a list of all available attributes while the panel on the right shows all additional attributes. In this example, that panel is blank. Between these two panels are, arrayed top to bottom, a single, right arrow, a double right arrow, and a single left arrow. The single arrows are disabled in this example.
